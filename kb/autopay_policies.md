\# AutoPay Policies



\## Eligibility Requirements

\- Account age >= 90 days

\- No NSF in last 6 months

\- Credit Card vs BOA differences apply



\## Common Issues: "Not Eligible for AutoPay"

Causes:

\- New account

\- Prior NSF flag



Resolution:

1\. Verify policy flags on account.

2\. Share steps to qualify.

3\. Offer manual payment alternatives.
