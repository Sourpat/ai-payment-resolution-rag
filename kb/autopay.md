\# AutoPay Policies



\## Eligibility Requirements

\- Account age ≥ 90 days

\- No NSF in last 6 months

\- Tokenized payment method required

\- BOA allowed only if account is in good standing



\## Common Issues

\### AUTOPAY\_NOT\_ELIGIBLE

\*\*Causes\*\*

\- New account

\- NSF history

\- Missing tokenized card



\*\*Resolution Steps\*\*

1\. Check eligibility flags

2\. Share steps to qualify

3\. Offer manual payment



\*\*Agent Script\*\*

> This account isn’t eligible for AutoPay yet due to policy checks. I’ll share steps to qualify.
