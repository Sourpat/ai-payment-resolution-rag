\# Statement Reference Matching



\### STATEMENT\_REFERENCE\_ERROR



\*\*Causes\*\*

\- Invalid reference

\- ERP sync delay



\*\*Resolution Steps\*\*

1\. Re-fetch statements from ERP

2\. Ask for correct reference

3\. Retry mapping
