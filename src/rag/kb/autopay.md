# AutoPay Policies
## Eligibility Requirements
- Account age >= 90 days
- No NSF in last 6 months
- BOA vs Credit Card rules differ; CC accounts may have additional checks.

## Common Issues
### "Not Eligible for AutoPay"
**Causes**: recent NSF, new account, CC-only restrictions.
**Resolution Steps**:
1) Check eligibility service / policy flags
2) Communicate steps to qualify
3) Offer manual payment / ACH as alternatives
